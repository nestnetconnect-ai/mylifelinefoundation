Who We Are

About My Lifeline Foundation

A grassroots non-profit committed to lasting change in Odisha's most underserved communities.

👁

Our Vision

A society where every individual — regardless of geography or circumstance — has equal access to education, health, and opportunity to thrive.

🎯

Our Mission

To mobilise compassionate youth volunteers and strategic partnerships to deliver measurable, sustainable impact at the grassroots level across Odisha.

7+Years Active
30Districts
100%Volunteer-Led

Core Values

What We Do

Our Core Programs

Three verticals. One unified mission — uplift Odisha's forgotten corners.

01
📚

Education

Free tutoring centres, scholarship drives, digital literacy camps, and school supply distributions across 14 districts.

  • After-school learning centres
  • Merit scholarships for students
  • Digital literacy workshops
  • Library & Book Donation Drive
1,200+ students benefited
Core Focus
02
🩹

Health

Free medical camps, blood donation drives, mental health awareness, and hygiene kit distributions in rural blocks.

  • Free medical & dental camps
  • Blood donation drives
  • Maternal health awareness
  • Mental health sensitization
8,500+ patients served
03
🌿

Rural Development

Livelihood training, women empowerment SHGs, clean water initiatives, and sustainable agriculture support.

  • Skill & vocational training
  • Women self-help groups
  • Clean water & sanitation
  • Sustainable farming support
340+ families supported
